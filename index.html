JH Eagles

A redesigned version of the EagleTime app


Features

Performance

JH Eagles has improved speed and performance.

Design

New modernized design follows Material Design 3.

Interaction

Easy-to-use interface allows you to quickly navigate through the app.

Fast Performance

JH Eagles is much faster than the original EagleTime app. The app loads faster and consumes less memory.

Tested on Galaxy S20+.
Actual performance may vary depending on your device.

Modernized Design

The EagleTime app uses Material Design 2, which is now outdated.

JH Eagles uses Material Design 3, also known as Material You, to make the app look more modernized and fit in with Android 12+ devices.

Material Design 3 apps have an accent color that changes according to your device's wallpaper. JH Eagles allows the user to customize the accent color of the app to their own liking.

Below, you can see what the app will look like with different accent colors and themes.

Dark theme

User Friendly

JH Eagles is easy to use with clearly visible buttons and menus. There are no hard-to-find features hidden in sub-menus, everything that is available is just one click away.

The bottom tab navigation allows fast navigation between screens in the app.


About

JH Eagles is a utility app made for students at Johnston Heights Secondary.
It is based on the original EagleTime app, made by Appazur.
Redesigned with Material Design 3 to match with Android 12+ phones.
